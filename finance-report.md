# Finance Report Metadata Specification

## 1. Asset Identification

-   Ticker Symbol
-   Exchange
-   ISIN / CUSIP
-   Asset Type (Stock / ETF)
-   Base Currency
-   Trading Currency
-   Sector / Industry
-   Country / Region Exposure

## 2. Market Overview

-   Current Price
-   Market Capitalization / Assets Under Management
-   52 Week High / Low
-   Average Daily Volume
-   Valuation Multiples (P/E, P/B, EV/EBITDA where applicable)
-   Dividend Yield (if applicable)

## 3. Performance Metrics (Standardized for Comparison)

### Required Time Horizons

-   1 Month
-   3 Month
-   YTD
-   1 Year
-   3 Year (Annualized)
-   5 Year (Annualized)
-   10 Year (Annualized or Since Inception)

### Required Calculations

-   Total Return (price + dividends)
-   Annualized Return
-   Volatility (Standard Deviation)
-   Maximum Drawdown
-   Sharpe Ratio (if risk-free proxy available)
-   Tracking Error (ETFs only)

## 4. Holdings & Exposure (ETF or Portfolio View)

-   Number of Holdings
-   Top 10 Holdings with Weights
-   Sector Allocation (%)
-   Geographic Allocation (%)
-   Market Cap Breakdown (Large / Mid / Small)
-   Concentration Risk Metrics (Top 5 / Top 10 % weight)

## 5. Financial Strength & Outstanding Debt (Stocks)

### Required Debt Metrics

-   Total Debt
-   Short-Term Debt
-   Long-Term Debt
-   Net Debt
-   Debt-to-Equity Ratio
-   Interest Coverage Ratio
-   Debt Maturity Profile (if available)
-   Credit Rating (if available)

### Required Cash Flow Metrics

-   Operating Cash Flow
-   Free Cash Flow
-   Cash & Cash Equivalents
-   Liquidity Ratios (Current Ratio, Quick Ratio)

## 6. ETF Structural Metrics (ETFs)

-   Expense Ratio
-   Tracking Method (Physical / Synthetic)
-   Replication Error
-   Fund Domicile
-   Distribution Policy (Accumulating / Distributing)
-   Rebalancing Frequency
-   Index Provider

## 7. Growth Prospects Framework

### Macro Drivers

-   Economic Growth Trends
-   Interest Rate Sensitivity
-   Inflation Sensitivity
-   Regulatory Environment
-   Geopolitical Exposure

### Industry Drivers

-   Industry Growth Rate
-   Competitive Landscape
-   Technology Adoption
-   Supply Chain Resilience
-   Market Share Trends

### Company / Fund Specific Drivers

-   Revenue Growth Forecasts
-   Earnings Growth Forecasts
-   Capital Investment Plans
-   Innovation Pipeline
-   Pricing Power
-   Cost Efficiency Programs

### Risk Factors

-   Currency Risk
-   Political Risk
-   Market Cyclicality
-   Liquidity Risk
-   Regulatory Changes
-   Technological Disruption

### Scenario Analysis

-   Bull Case Assumptions
-   Base Case Assumptions
-   Bear Case Assumptions
-   Expected CAGR Range

-   Growth Score (1--10)
-   Risk Score (1--10)
-   Valuation Score (1--10)
-   Income Score (1--10)
-   Diversification Score (1--10)
-   Cost Efficiency Score (ETFs only)
